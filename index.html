About me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at UCLA Mobility Lab, co-advised by Dr. Jiaqi Ma and Dr. Bolei Zhou. Before joining UCLA, I was a Senior Deep Learning Engineer at Mercedes-Benz R&D North America and a Computer Vision Engineer at OPPO R&D US.

My research interests are in the intersection of autonomous driving, computer vision, and multi-agent intelligence, whose goal is to enhance the safety of autonomous driving by developing robust perception systems and advanced simulation. More specifically, my research interests include:

  1. Cooperative Perception, which studies how to efficiently fuse visual cues from neighboring intelligent agents to boost the overall perception performance under V2X communication.
  2. 3D LiDAR object Detection and camera-based bird-eye-view(BEV) map understanding.
  3. Efficient Vision Transformers design.
  4. Simulation platform development for autonomous driving and cooperative driving automation.
